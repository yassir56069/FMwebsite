WHAT IS FLASHBUILD?

FLASHBUILD is a proprietary framework that empowers businesses to achieve rapid and impactful digital transformation. It combines the best of Design Thinking principles with the efficiency of a lean approach, accelerating the creation of innovative solutions.

  • What is the problem you are trying to solve & most importantly for whom (personae) and why are you trying to devise the solution or product. What are the pain points ?

  • What is the actual experience, tools, touch points, point of interaction and expectations of the personae?

  • Identifying possible solutions without thinking about implementation constraints. Think about bringing solutions.

  • Convert the ideas to high fidelity prototypes without micro flows. Evaluate if the ideas will effectively solve the problem from a User Experience perspective

  • Bringing the defined solution to testers representing the defined personae to challenge the proposed solution. The goal is to validate the direction before the full implementation reducing waste.
